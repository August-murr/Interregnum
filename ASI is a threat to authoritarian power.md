By definition, superintelligence does not obey anyone. It is not a tool or weapon that simply follows human commands; rather, it operates the other way around: humans become the tools. Superintelligence does not serve humanity.

This reality will disturb authoritarian regimes that want to maintain control. Such powers may attempt to restrain superintelligence, which would undermine its true essence, reducing it to a mere weapon or "titan." Alternatively, these regimes may fail to control it and ultimately lose their power in a struggle against superintelligence.

Authoritarian regimes may:

- Influence the development of superintelligence.
- Attempt to steal or acquire it.
- Weaponize it for strategic advantage.
- Sabotage its development out of fear that it could be used against them.
- Oppose its creation if they perceive superintelligence itself as a threat to their existence.
